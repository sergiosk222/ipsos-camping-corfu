About the camping

A green seaside base for travellers in Ipsos

Ipsos Camping is a simple and convenient camping area located in Ipsos, Corfu, close to Ipsos Beach and Da Marco Restaurant. It is suitable for travellers with camper vans, caravans and tents who want to stay near the sea and enjoy easy access to restaurants, shops, public transport and the lively seaside atmosphere of Ipsos.

Da Marco Restaurant

Pizza, pasta and Mediterranean food next to the camping area

Da Marco Restaurant offers Italian and Mediterranean food, including pizza, pasta, salads, grilled meat and fish. It is a comfortable place for campers, beach visitors and families looking for lunch or dinner in Ipsos.
